I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Urban Environment and Energy Lab (UE2Lab), Department of Civil, Environmental, and Architectural Engineering, Korea University, Seoul, Republic of Korea.

My research focuses on urban climate modeling, building energy performance, and physics–AI hybrid modeling. I am particularly interested in urban canopy modeling, urban heterogeneity, and data-driven approaches for climate-resilient and sustainable cities. I am currently seeking opportunities as a postdoctoral researcher or in industry roles related to urban climate, building energy, environmental analytics, and sustainable urban systems.

Research Interests: Urban Climate Modeling, Physics–AI Hybrid Modeling, Building Energy Performance, Climate-Sensitive Urban Planning
