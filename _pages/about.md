Hi, my name is Gigih Rahmandhani Setyantho, or **Gigih** (he/him). I am a final-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Civil, Environmental, and Architectural Engineering at Korea University ([고려대학교 건축사회환경공학과](https://ace.korea.ac.kr/index.php)), where I conduct research in the [Urban Energy and Environment Laboratory (UE2Lab)](https://ue2lab.korea.ac.kr/) under the supervision of [Prof. Yeonsook Heo](https://ace.korea.ac.kr/bbs/board.php?bo_table=eng2_1&wr_id=27). 

My research focuses on **urban climate**, **urban wind environments**, and **building performance** through physics-based simulation and data-driven approaches. I am particularly interested in developing robust and interpretable modeling frameworks that support climate-resilient urban planning and sustainable built environments. My recent work explores **hybrid physics–AI approaches** to improve urban wind speed predictions under real-world conditions. For a summary of my education, experiences, and publications, please see my [2-page résumé](https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/pj9g9xpeme0iaz56skwzn/2-pageResume_Gigih_260622.pdf?rlkey=0esloeqtvjaw8u62pbvbqucme&st=qk322cn0&dl=0). I welcome conversations on research collaborations and future opportunities related to urban climate and sustainable built environments.
